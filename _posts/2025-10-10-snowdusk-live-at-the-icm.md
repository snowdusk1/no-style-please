October 2nd, 2025 - 4:30pm - 8:30pm PDT
Address: 998 Industry Dr. Tukwila, WA 98188
(South of Seattle near SEATAC Airport)
For more information about the museum go to [https://icm.museum/?visit](https://icm.museum/?visit)
